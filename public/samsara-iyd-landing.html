About the Event

A Global Celebration
of Harmony

International Yoga Day, proclaimed by the United Nations on June 21 each year, is a worldwide recognition of yoga's universal appeal and its profound contributions to health, well-being, and inner peace.

Samsara Wellness, in partnership with the Ministry of Ayush, Government of India, presents a grand celebration bringing world-class yoga masters, wellness workshops, meditation sessions, and cultural events to communities across India and beyond.

The 2026 theme — "Yoga for One Earth, One Health" — reflects the unity of all humanity through yoga's ancient wisdom.
